About Our Project

We are aiming to create a VR touring tool that works seamlessly through a website. Users can submit their own 3D models of spaces in either FBX or OBJ formats. Our website will process these models and convert them into fully interactive VR scenes that users can explore freely.

Our innovative platform eliminates the need for external applications or complex software installations. Everything happens directly in your browser, making virtual reality experiences accessible to everyone with a VR headset and internet connection.

Whether you're a hotel owner wanting to showcase your property, a real estate agent displaying properties, or a museum curator offering virtual tours, our tool provides a simple yet powerful solution for creating immersive VR experiences.
